Prestige

Centerpiece + Silverware organizer.

 

Toolbox project designed to beautify the dining experience and grant guests the honor at the table.

 

Abstract

When setting a dinner table, the silverware and plates are already placed for you, however, depending on the meal being eaten, the types of utensils used can vary for person to person. Especially in South Asian culture, the served food can be eaten with or without utensils. It does not even matter if a spoon or fork is being used at certain times, so it leads to everyone having their own preference.

 

Goal

Prestige is a product that gives the guest that preference. Not only does it beautify the dinning table, it honors the guest by granting them the ability to choose their silverware.
